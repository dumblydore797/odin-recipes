Homemade Lasagna

Here's a unique homemade lasagna recipe featuring a rich, from-scratch meaty tomato sauce and a delightfully cheesy filling. Instead of the usual ricotta, this recipe uses a combination of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan for a flavorful twist. Pair it with a fresh green salad and some crusty garlic bread for a perfect meal.

Making homemade lasagna is easier than you think. Don't feel intimidated by the layers of delicious Italian flavors—give this homemade lasagna recipe a try today!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Begin by browning and crumbling the ground meat in a skillet. Add the onion and cook until translucent. Then, incorporate the canned tomato products along with half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. While the sauce simmers, boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they reach al dente consistency.
  3. Create the cheese layer by combining cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna by layering the ingredients as directed in the recipe—begin with a sauce layer and conclude with mozzarella.
  5. Once assembled, cover the lasagna with foil and bake in the preheated oven for approximately 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking until the top develops a golden-brown hue.
