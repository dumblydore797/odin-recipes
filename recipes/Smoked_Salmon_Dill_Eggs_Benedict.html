Smoked Salmon Dill Eggs Benedict

Delight in a Luxurious Brunch Classic: Smoked Salmon Eggs Benedict

Imagine waking up to the comforting aroma of freshly toasted English muffins, topped with luscious slices of smoked salmon and perfectly poached eggs, all enveloped in a zesty dill-infused butter. This Eggs Benedict variation is a culinary masterpiece that balances rich flavors with delicate textures

Ingredients

  • butter
  • fresh dill
  • lemon zest
  • cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • white vinegar
  • salt
  • eggs
  • English muffins, split and toasted
  • sliced smoked salmon
  • small fresh dill sprigs

Steps

  1. To craft this culinary masterpiece, start by blending softened butter with aromatic fresh dill, a hint of lemon zest, and a touch of cayenne pepper for a subtle kick. This dill-infused butter becomes the hero of our dish, adding a burst of herbaceous goodness to each bite.
  2. Next, prepare the eggs with care. Poaching them to perfection is an art - the whites should be firm yet yielding, and the yolks thickened but irresistibly runny. Each egg is gently lifted from the simmering water and carefully placed atop a buttered English muffin half.
  3. Layer on the star of the show: slices of premium smoked salmon. The silky, smoky flavor of the salmon complements the richness of the poached egg, creating a harmonious blend of tastes.
  4. Finally, season with a sprinkle of cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to enhance the ensemble. Garnish with a fresh dill sprig for a pop of color and herbaceous freshness.
  5. Indulge in this luxurious brunch creation - a feast for the senses that promises to elevate your morning routine and leave you craving more. Serve with a side of sunshine and a cup of your favorite morning brew for the ultimate breakfast experience.
